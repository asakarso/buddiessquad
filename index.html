GFRIEND

GFRIEND is a girl group from South Korea consisted with six members and debut on January, 2015. They made their debut with the EP Season of Glass. GFriend won several 2015 female rookie awards and has garnered momentum since their debut despite being from a small company. During their fan-meeting after the album's showcase release, they announced their fandom name to be "Buddy".
